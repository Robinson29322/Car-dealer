Safari

Engine & Performance

Engine (Diesel only – Kryotec):

* 2.0L Turbo Diesel (4-cylinder, FCA-sourced Kryotec)
* ~170 bhp, 350 Nm torque

Transmission

* 6-speed Manual
* 6-speed Torque Converter Automatic

Drivetrain:

* Front-Wheel Drive (FWD)

Mileage

* ~14–16.3 km/l (ARAI & real-world mixed conditions)
* ~13–15 km/l (real-world city/highway approx)

Dimensions & Build

* Length: ~4668 mm
* Width: ~1922 mm
* Height: ~1795 mm
* Wheelbase: ~2741 mm
* Ground clearance: ~200 mm
* Seating: 6 / 7-seater options
* Body: Full-size monocoque SUV
* Boot space: ~420–680 liters (depending on 3rd-row use)

Interior & Features

Infotainment

* 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system (top variants)
* Android Auto & Apple CarPlay (wireless support in higher trims)
* Premium JBL sound system (higher variants)

Comfort

* Dual / 3-zone automatic climate control (variant-based)
* Ventilated front seats
* Panoramic sunroof
* Powered driver seat with memory function
* Captain seat option (6-seater configuration)

Tech

* Fully digital instrument cluster
* Wireless charging
* Air purifier
* Connected car tech (Tata iRA system)
* Ambient lighting
* Drive modes (Eco / City / Sport)
* 360° camera

Safety

* 6 airbags (standard in most variants)
* ABS + EBD
* ESP (Electronic Stability Program)
* Hill Hold Control
* Traction control system
* TPMS
* ISOFIX child seat mounts
* Rear parking sensors & camera
* ADAS Level 2 (top trims)
* 5-star Bharat NCAP safety rating
