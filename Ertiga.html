Ertiga

Engine & Performance

Engine (ICE variants):

* 1.5L Naturally Aspirated Petrol (K15C Smart Hybrid, 4-cylinder)
* ~101–103 bhp, 136.8 Nm torque

Transmission

* 5-speed Manual
* 6-speed Torque Converter Automatic

Drivetrain:

* Front-Wheel Drive (FWD)

Mileage

* ~20–21 km/l (Petrol MT real-world approx)
* ~18–20 km/l (Petrol AT real-world approx)
* ~26 km/kg (CNG variant approx)

Dimensions & Build

* Length: ~4395 mm
* Wheelbase: ~2740 mm
* Ground clearance: ~185 mm
* Seating: 7-seater
* Body: MPV
* Boot space: ~209 liters (all seats up), expandable with folded rows

Interior & Features

Infotainment

* 7-inch SmartPlay Pro touchscreen
* Android Auto & Apple CarPlay (wired)
* 4–6 speaker audio system (variant dependent)

Comfort

* Automatic climate control
* Rear AC vents (roof-mounted for 2nd & 3rd row)
* Push-button start/stop
* Height-adjustable driver seat
* Sliding/reclining 2nd row

Tech

* Semi-digital instrument cluster
* Connected car tech (Suzuki Connect – higher variants)
* Steering-mounted controls
* Cruise control (higher variants)

Safety

* 4 airbags (newer variants; older trims had 2 airbags)
* ABS + EBD
* ESP (standard in latest models)
* Hill Hold Assist (AT variants)
* Rear parking sensors & camera
* ISOFIX child seat mounts

Price

Indian Standard

* ₹8.7 lakh – ₹13.2 lakh (ex-showroom)

At Car-e Port

* ₹6 lakh – ₹11.5 lakh (on-road)
